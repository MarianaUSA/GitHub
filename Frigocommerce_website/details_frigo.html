Compressor

These original compressors for domestic refrigeration are manufactured by ACC (Electrolux), the leader in compressor manufacturing, and are guaranteed to have a long malfunction-free operating lifespan, low power consumption and very low noise levels.

The AS series are new compressors, manufactured by Verdichter - Austria with a design based on that of the series OF (OF789, OF1033, OF1350). These compressors are specifically designed for servicing purposes - they can work with R134a, R406a, R409a, MP39 and R12 without requiring the change of oil, which allows services to maintain a smaller quantity of universal compressors on stock. The compressors are characterized by their high dependability, long operation lifespan and very low noise levels.
